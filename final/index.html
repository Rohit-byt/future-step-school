What kids actually going to love

Adaptive and fun learning

Adaptive and fun learning for kids is an exciting educational approach that caters to young learners’ individual needs and preferences. At The Future Step School we combine personalized content, interactive activities, and engaging challenges to create a positive and enjoyable learning environment for kids. Children explore topics through games, collaborate on projects, and receive real-time feedback—making education not only effective but also delightful! 

Sports facilities

Sports facilities at The Future Step School play a crucial role in promoting physical activity, teamwork, and overall well-being a​mong students. Remember, purpose of sports facilities isn’t just about winning medals; it’s about shaping well-rounded individuals who thrive physically, mentally, and socially.

Supportive Teacher Staff

G ood school teacher staff is essential for creating a positive and effective learning environment. 
Passion for Teaching: At The Future Step Schoo l staff is passionate about education and genuinely care about the success and well-being of the students. They inspire and motivate students to learn, fostering a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

Facilities

Digital classroom

In today’s digital age, integrating technology into education is essential. At The Future Step School embracing digital classrooms empowers our teachers and enriches students’ learning journeys. Digital classrooms use Interactive sessions, animated videos, and 3D models to engage students and simplify complex concepts. Students can learn at their own pace, access resources anytime, and review content as needed.

CCTV Monitored Classrooms

CCTV surveillance at The Future Step School guarantees safer and more secure school environment, benefiting studen​ts, staff, administrators and more importantly peace of mind for parents.

Responsible citizen/Human being

At The Future Step School teaching kids to be responsible citizens and human beings is an essential part of school curriculum. Responsible citizens and human beings are those who care about themselves, others, and the environment, and who act in ways that reflect their values and principles. The Future Step Schools plays a vital role in fostering responsibility, humanity and integrity among students.

School owned transport for kids safety

The Future Step School has its own transport which is a safe and smart choice for kids and their parents. It ensures that kids reach school and home safely, conveniently, and comfortably. It also helps kids develop positive attitudes and habits towards traveling and learning. School owned transport is not just a means of transport, but a means of education and empowerment.

About us

The Future step school is venture of Kakeshwar Vidya Prasarak Sanstha Bhaler which is working towards betterment and empowerment of village students since last 35 years. The school will open new era for the village students who want to persue global opportunities. The school will provide Education in English medium maintaining cultural roots intact by incorporating ancient Gurukul education system. This will make sure students will face the world with confidence without compromising their cultural values.
